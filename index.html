Why are we frequently named one of the"Best Restaurants in Montauk"

Located in Montauk, Westlake Fish House is the go-to place for fresh Sushi, 2019 award winning New England chowder, and the infamous Westlake Burger. We are located in the Westlake Marina, right beside the docks, and our proximity to the water should assure you that the seafood we serve is as fresh as possible.

We love our customers, and we loved to provide you with a great casual dining experience combined with the best seafood in Montauk.
